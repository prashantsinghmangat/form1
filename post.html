    Vacancy Details for Army Recruitment Rally Meerut Online Form 2018

  • Post Name – Soldier General Duty, Soldier Technical, Soldier Clerk/ Store Keeper Technical / Inventory Management, Soldier Nursing Assistant, Soldier Tradesmen

  • Category wise VacancyCategory wise Vacancy – As Per Rules

  • Pay Scale –Pay Scale – As Per Rules

  • Educational Qualification

  • Soldier GD – SSLC/ Matric with 45 % in aggregate and 33% in each subject

  • Soldier Technical – 10+2 Intermediate in Science with Physics, Chemistry, Math and English with 50 % marks in aggregate and 40% in each subject

  • Soldier Nursing Assistant – 10+2 Intermediate pass with English and Physics, Chemistry, Biology with 50 % marks in aggregate and 40% in each subject

  • Soldier Clerk/ Store Keeper Technical / Inventory Management – 10+2 Intermediate in any stream with English and Maths/Account/Book Keeping as a subjects with 60% in aggregate and 50% in each subject

  • Soldier Tradesman – 10th or 8th pass /ITI

  • How to Apply for Army Recruitment Rally Meerut Online Form 2018 – Candidates can apply online through the official website of Join Indian Army or may click on the Apply Online link from the below box.
